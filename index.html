Diagnosis:

  • Initial test is 24 hour urinary 5-hydroxyindoleacetic acid. (5-HIAA)
    • Foods containing serotonin (banana, pineapple, avocado, tomato, avocado) etc should be avoided.
  • Also plasma chromogranin A y
  • Can also use plasma level of 5-hydroxyindoleacetic acid.

Management:

  • For temporary symptomatic relief while waiting for surgical treatment -> octreotide; somatostatin analog which act on the neutroendocrine cell somatostatin receptors and inhibit serotonin release.
  • Somatostain itself has a short half life lasting minutes. Octreotide and other analogs have been made to have longer half lives (upto 2 hours) Source
  • Some actions of somatostatin (and octreotide) (From notes search results)
    • Inhibits GH secretion
    • Causes splanchnic vasoconstriction
    • Inhibits gastric acid secretion
    • Antagonizes effects of sulfonylurea.
    • Symptomatic management of carcinoid syndrome.
  • The variety of actions of somatostatin can be explained by the tissue distribution of the 5 types of somatostatin receptor.

[!INFO] Background info on Serotonin: Skip

It has been known since the mid-nineteenth century that after blood clots the resulting serum possesses a substance that constricts vascular smooth muscle so as to increase vascular tone. Around the turn of this century, platelets were identified as the source of this substance. In the late 1940s, Rapport and collaborators [1] isolated, purified and identified this “tonic” substance in “serum” (hence, serotonin) as the substituted indole 5-hydroxytryptamine
Platelets don't synthesize their own serotonin; they actively take it up from plasma.

ADAMTS degrades the vWF multimers and prevents thrombus formation particularly in high sheer stress areas like small arterioles and capillaries. Shear stress leads to a conformational change in the large VWF multimers that exposes the ADAMTS13 cleavage site

  • The major cause of severe ADAMTS13 deficiency is an autoantibody. Inherited gene mutations account for a minority.

  • Patients with ADAMTS13 deficiency usually get TTP when they encounter a TRIGGER such as infection or pregnancy.
