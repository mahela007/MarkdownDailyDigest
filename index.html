Burkitt lymphoma

  • Classicaly presents as cheek swelling.
  • Caused by EBV.
  • Pathogensis: chromosome 8:14 transolcation.
  • Translocation of cMYC gene to IgG heavy chain locus.
  • B cells begin expressing large amounts of growth regulatory protein which results in rapid cell proliferation.
  • Very Rapidly growing cancer.
  • Can be treated effectively with chemo if identified early.
