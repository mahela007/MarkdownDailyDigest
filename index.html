Mnemonic: 3 Rules of dynamic auscultation

  1. Increased preload increases left sided murmurs (except VSD) (and decreases HOCM and MVP).
  2. Increased afterload increases the left sided regurgitant murmurs (AR, MR) and VSD and (decreases HOCM and MVP).
  3. Increasing afterload OR preload decreases HOCM and MVP murmur.
  4. And of course, the inverse of these is true as well.

S3 Vs. S4 - Third Vs. Fourth Heart sounds

[!TIP] Mnemonic: S3 - lose and early, S4 - Stiff and late.

" A young man will arrive early cos he can walk, and old man will be stiff and late"

S3 - compliant ventricle, early diastole (occurs in volume overload)
S4 - stiff ventricle, late diastole.
