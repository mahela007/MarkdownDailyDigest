Frank-Starling curves

Mechanism of the Frank starling effect:
It is not simply due to increased overlap of the actin and myosin filaments with increased myocardial stretching.
It has been shows that increased myocardial stretching leads to

  1. increased affinity of troponin for calcium
  2. myocardial calcium handling.

In addition to increased overlap, these effects increase contractile force. Source
