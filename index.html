Panhypopituitarism

#2023BSQ Q34
Panhypopituitarism = "deficiency of all anterior pituitary hormones"
Posterior pituitary is usually spared in pan hypopituitarism.

Cortisol and TSH are the last to be affected. (FL-AT-PEG) That's a good thing because they are "essential for life"
In hypopituitarism, mineralocorticoids do no need to be replaced.
Kallman syndrome -> isolated GnRH deficiency -> isolated GH defieciency.

Causes of pan hypopiutarism:

Commonest: Tumour, apoplexy (haemorrhage into a tumor), RT, surgery / trauma

Effects of panhypopituitarism

Hypopituitarism can affect a single hormone.
but mulitiple hormones are usually affected :

  • F - Loss of libido, loss of secondary sexual hair, amenorrhoea, erectile dysfunction, osteoporosis
  • L
  • A - hyponatremia, hypotension, addisonian crisis during illness
  • T - "A&T" are last to be affected but T- hypothyroidism
  • P - hyperprolactinaemia
  • E - Endorphins -? effect in panhypopituitarism
  • G - growth failure in children and "impaired wellbeing" in adults.

Management of panhypopituitarism

  • F & L - male: testosterone oral or IM, female: [[Hormone Physiology#Oestrogen|oestrogen]] and progesterone;
    • If fertility is needed - gonadotrophins (GnRH) must be replaced
  • A - Usually no need to replace mineralocorticoids. Glucocorticoids (essential for life) replaced with hydrocortisone 10,5,5 dose. (morning, lunch evening);
  • T - Essential for life ; Levothyroxine - synthetic T4. Only to be started after normal ACTH axis function is restored -> ! addisonian crisis if not.
  • P - Dopamine agonist - cabergoline
  • E -
  • G - GH routinely replaced in children; Replacement recommended for adults who have quality of life impairment.

Diagnosis of latent TB

Two tests:

  1. Tuberculin skin test; Mantoux method
  2. IGRA

Mantoux test:

Positive test only identifies TB infection. NOT TB disease.
Intradermal tuberculin stimulates a delayed-type hypersensitivity response mediated by T lymphocytes and, in patients with prior mycobacterial exposure, causes induration at the injection site within 48 to 72 hours.
Previous BCG administration and tuberculin test:

  • receipt of BCG within first year of life -> low level (<10mm positivity, possible life long)
  • BCG vaccination given later -> higher reactivity to skin test, for about 10 years after vaccination.

IGRA

Measures in vitro release of interferon gamma from patients cells in response to TB antigen.
Not affected by BCG vaccination.
More expensive than Mantoux.
