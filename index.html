Bacillus anthracis - gram positive rod with tripartite toxin causing

  1. Oedema
  2. Macrophage toxin
  3. Protective antigen.

Farmers and those exposed to animals are at risk.
Also presents with a painless eschar with surrounding oedema but no pus.
