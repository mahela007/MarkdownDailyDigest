[!INFO] Onion skin fibrosis:

  • Liver transplantation is the only treatment than can alter progression. Drugs won't work.
    - But ursodeoxycholic is the most promising agent. Source
    - Pruritus can be reduced with cholestyramine.
    • PSC without IBD is more likely to occur in women.
  • hypergammaglobulinaemia is present.
  • ultimately leads to cirrhosis.
  • Complications: Osteoporosis (fat soluble vitamins), cholangiocarcinoma, cholangitis.

  • Langerhans cells are antigen presenting cells but the abnormal cells in the disease have lower antigen presenting ability.
  • Birbeck granules present on EM: They have a "tennis racket" shape.
  • Is one cause of interstitial lung disease.
  • The disease involves the formation of multiple granulomas throughout the body.
    Adults commonly have single system involvement.
    • Only skin involvement -> Good prognosis; resection of local lesion can be curative.
    • Lung involvement -> bad prognosis
  • pituitary involvement -> commonest manifestation is diabetes insipidus.
  • Adults commonly have lytic lesions in the jaw and skull.
  • Upper and middle zone involvement is characteristic.
  • bone pain, typically in the skull or proximal femur
