Haemostasis

  1. The quickest response to endothelial damage is formation of the platelet plug. Platelets roll off the exposed collagen and stick to it.
  • Low dose aspirin irreversibly inhibits COX-1(and also COX-2) -> inhibits Thromboxane A2 production. See [[Coagulation#^818075|prostacyclin]]
  • Clopidogrel (which is a prodrug) and prasugrel irreversibly inhibit the binding of adenosine to P2Y12 receptors
  • Ticagrelor produces non-competitive reversible inhibition.
  • They inhibit adenosine mediated platelet aggregation.
  • Clopidogrel is the prodrug. It's activated by the CYP2C19 enzyme system which can be inhibited by omeprazole!. Don't co administer the drugs.
