Carotid barrorecetors

They are more sensitive to pulsatile pressure than constant pressure.
Widening of pulse pressue while mantaining the same MAP causes increased baroreceptor discharge. Maximal discharge occurs at pressure of 200mmHg. No Activity below 50mmHg.
The "gradient" of discharge frequency Vs. BP increases with decreasing mean pressure.

[!INFO] i.e lower the map, the more the response to wide pulse pressure.
