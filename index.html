fibromuscular dysplasia:

  • Fibromuscular dysplasia (FMD) is an angiopathy that affects medium-sized arteries predominantly in young women of childbearing age.
  • normal arterial smooth muscle is replaced with more inelastic tissue
  • can cause renal impairment or strokes/ TIA
