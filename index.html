- Most specific rash is the heliotrope rash but it's present only in a minority.
- Gottron's papules : red, scaly bumps overlying fingers and knuckles. May be associated with similar rash on elbows and knees.
- V sign - similar to shawl sign but along the neckline of clothes.
- 20% of those with PM or DM have anti tRNA synthetase antibodies and are more likely to get pulmonary interstitial fibrosis, Raynaud's phenomenon, and "mechanic's hands - roughening and cracking of skin".
- Respiratory muscles are affected -> respiratory failure;
- Oesophageal muscle involvement -> dysphagia.

  • creatine kinase, aminotransferases, LDH and aldolase are all raised.
  • Mainstay of treatment is prednisilone, continued for at least 1 month after clinical and biochemical resolution of myositis.
  • Azathioprine, ciclosporin, cyclophosphamide and MMF are all used as steroid sparing agents.
  • IVIG in refractory cases.
