Anti-platelet treatment in post-acute STEMI management

  1. Twelve Month DAPT remains the default for STEMI patients.
    1. Preferred drugs for this are prasugrel and ticagrelor.
  2. In High bleeding risk patients, abbreviated DAPT strategies or DAPT deescalation strageties can be used.
    1. De-escalation means switching from prasugrel / ticagrelor to clopidogrel.
