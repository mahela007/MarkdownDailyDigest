A few points on basic physiology

Innervation of bronchial smooth muscle

Innervation of bronchial smooth muscle is predominantly parasympathetic, not sympathetic.
Cholinergic parasympathetic nerves mediate bronchospasm while noncholinergic parasympathetic nerves mediate bronchodilatation.

Circadian rhythms in asthma

#2022GM-MAY Q14

Asthma is a disease with a strong circadian rhythm; it is characteristic of asthma that symptoms worsen in the early hours of the morning around 4:00Â am.
Both FEV1 and PEF vary in a circadian manner in healthy individuals with a nadir at approximately 4:00 am. However, in asthma, the amplitude of the circadian rhythm of both FEV1 and PEF is greatly magnified.

Definitions:
Forced inspiratory vital capacity:

The maximal volume of air which can be inspired during forced inspiration from a position of full expiration.

FIV1:

volume of air inspired in the first second during forced inspiration from full expiration.

Forced expiration increases intra airway pressure and widens extrathoracic airways. However, forced inspiration conversely causes the extrthoracic airways to narrow to the the negative intraluminal pressure.
Accordingly extrathoracic airway obstruction is best detected during a forced inspiration
