Renal blood flow

Facts about renal blood flow:

Source

  • Renal arteries are end arteries. [[whatIsAnEndArtery.png]]
  • There is a high pressure capillary bed (glomerular) and low pressure capillary bed (peritubular).
  • 95% of renal blood flow goes to the cortex! only 5% to the medulla.
  • Blood flow autoreglation works in the MAP range of 75 to 160.
