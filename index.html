Efficiency Bar - Gazette on PSC

Person't who cannot be recruited to government service

  1. Someone who isn't a citizen or has citizens rights
  2. A person who has served in the public sector but
    1. Has VOPed
    2. Compulsory retirement for general inefficiency
    3. Rertirement as merciful alternative to dismissal
    4. Dismissed
  3. Person convicted for a crime against the DSR or SL
  4. Any person convicted under the criminal procedure code
  5. Anyone declared as bankrupt

The efficiency bar

Requirement defined in scheme of recruitment / service minute

  1. Required for confirmation of appointment
  2. Promotion
  3. passing to a salary step

Conducting authority is defined in the above.

Form stating successful completion of the examination must be given to the candidate by appointing authority. Photocopy of form must be attached to application for salary increment.

Effective date of passingis the date of the examination.

If exam not passed by due date, HOI / HOD / Administrative authority / appointing authority will defer increments an inform officer of such.

If not passed, extension can be granted to officer, upon request by the PSC if the exam was delayed outside the officers control.

Release of a public officer

Release to another post

To be appointed to another post in the public service, officer must be release dfrom current post.
Application for release must be forwarded to appointing authority through head of depatment or head of institution

The request will be denied only if

  1. Officer is under a bond to serve in the current post
  2. officer has special training which is required for his current post
  3. Release would significantly disrupt the functioning of the department to an extent greater than the officer actually serving in the current post.

If an officer has received special training for his current post, he should be released only if this training is useful for his new post.

If selection to a new post requires training, officer will be relased for training
