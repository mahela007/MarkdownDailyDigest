A few points on basic physiology

Innervation of bronchial smooth muscle

Innervation of bronchial smooth muscle is predominantly parasympathetic, not sympathetic.

Circadian rhythms in asthma

#2022GM-MAY Q14

Asthma is a disease with a strong circadian rhythm; it is characteristic of asthma that symptoms worsen in the early hours of the morning around 4:00 am.
Both FEV1 and PEF vary in a circadian manner in healthy individuals with a nadir at approximately 4:00 am. However, in asthma, the amplitude of the circadian rhythm of both FEV1 and PEF is greatly magnified.
