#2020SBR-JUL/Q01

  • Drugs: NSAIDS, cephalosporins, PPIs and many other antibiotics.[[General Medicine 4#Q 5 Adverse effects of PPI use.]]
    • Usually no significant proteinuria but can be present.
    • Can present with proteinuria if the disease is NSAID induced.
  • Investigation : Urine white cell casts, eosinophiluria (but this is not sensitive or specific). Definitive diagnosis requires renal biopsy.
