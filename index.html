Requirements for Victoria

  1. To work as a health professional in Victoria, you must apply for and be registered with the relevant Australian National Board.
    (eg: medical board, midwifery board, nursing board etc)
    For most healthcare workers, the process is managed by Australian Health Practitioners Regulation Agency (Ahpra)

  2. International healthcare professionals may need to apply for a qualifications assessment for a skilled migration visa issued by the Australian Department of Home Affairs. This assessment process is separate and may be different from professional registration. Success in one does not guarantee success in the other.

  3. Recruitment happens directly through health and mental health and wellbeing services
    Apply for jobs through either

    1. careers.vic.gov.au
    2. Directly to the employer: list of victoria employers
