  1. outer membrane
  2. Intermediate peptidoglycan cell wall - which is thin
  3. Inner membrane

Gram +ve bacteria have two layers

  1. peptidoglycan cell wall - which is super thick
  2. inner membrane.

[!INFO] What is Viridans group streptococci?
Large group of commensal streptococcal Gram-positive bacteria species that are -hemolytic, producing a green coloration on blood agar plates
The term streptococcus viridans, refers to five groups of Streptococci that include Streptococcus anginosus group, mitis group, sanguinis group, salivarius group, and finally the mutans group
