[!Clinic] How to use the ophthalmoscope
From the BMJ: Source

  • Rest your thumb on the patients forehead (to lift the eye lid) and then rest your head on the thumb.
  • Go ahead and turn the focus wheel to bring things into focus. You don't need to leave it at 0.
  • Only the patient needs to take of their glasses.
  • The patient should be looking slightly inwards with the eye being examined.
  • The macula is seen temporal to the disk.
  • Have a sequence
    • Red reflex -> Optic disc -> Vessels -> Macula
